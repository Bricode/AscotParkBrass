Welcome to the home page of the Ascot part brass band. The Ascot Park Hotel Brass Band is an A Grade band and currently consists of 25 registered players. All players (whether just passing through town or permanent) are welcome to come along for a "blow". We practice at the Spey Street bandroom on Monday and Thursday evenings starting at 7.30pm.
